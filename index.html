— The Sundered Vale —

Two keeps. Two champions. One law: every monster you send to their gate fattens your coffers — every monster they send is yours to slay. Hold your line. Break theirs.

The Law of the Vale

The Loop

  • Send monsters (Barracks, B) — they march on the enemy castle and permanently raise your income (paid every 30s).
  • Defend your lane — your hero fights everything the enemy sends at you. Kills pay gold.
  • Spend gold: items at the Forge (G), training at the War Council (V), Keep upgrades to unlock Tier 2 & 3 monsters.
  • Raze the enemy castle to win.

Controls

  • W A S D move · mouse aims
  • Q W E R… actually Q E F abilities, R ultimate
  • 1–6 item actives · P pause · M mute

Wisdom

  • Income early beats items early — but a dead hero defends nothing.
  • Watch what the enemy buys. Golems shrug off magic; harpies fly over blades.
  • Priests, banners and necrothurges make a horde greater than its parts.
  • The Forge keeps secrets: hold the right pieces and they become something greater. The riddle book hints.
  • Falling behind grants Underdog's Favor. Castles below ¼ unleash arrow volleys. You are never out of it.
  • At minute 35 comes the Twilight — walls grow brittle, gates spawn faster, and the war ends.
